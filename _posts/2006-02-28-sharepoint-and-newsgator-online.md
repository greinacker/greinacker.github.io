> [*SharePointGator*](http://weblog.vb-tech.com/nick/archive/2006/02/27/1454.aspx)
>
> *I’d been working on this webpart ages ago to enter into the* <a href="http://www.newsgator.com/" target="_blank"><em>NewsGator</em></a> *API competition but didn’t end up getting it finished in time to submit it. Left it sitting in the projects folder for a couple of months until I decided to finish it this weekend as I was feeling a little ill and didn’t want to leave the house!*
>
> *So what’s it all about then? Well I called it SharePointGator (no doubt infringing on some copywrite), as it’s a NewsGator webpart for SharePoint. Using the* <a href="http://www.newsgator.com/ngs/api/default.aspx" target="_blank"><em>NewsGator webservice</em></a> *and your newsgator account details you can now view the rss feeds and unread blog posts from your newsgator account in SharePoint.*
