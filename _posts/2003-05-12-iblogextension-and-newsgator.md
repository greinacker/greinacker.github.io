I'm pleased to announce that NewsGator 1.2 will include full support for IBlogExtension, and we will simultaneously release plug-ins for most major weblog publishing tools.

> *Apparently the Blogger folks were first to the BlogThis name by about 4 years, so BlogThis is now* [*IBlogExtension*](http://www.pocketsoap.com/weblog/stories/2003/04/0023.html "http://www.pocketsoap.com/weblog/stories/2003/04/0023.html")*, and the interface definition has be revised based on earlier discussions between* [*Greg*](/gregr/weblog/2003/05/02/posting-interface-iblogextension/ "/gregr/weblog/2003/05/02/posting-interface-iblogextension/")*,* [*Luke*](http://www.hutteman.com/weblog/2003/05/02.html#000079 "http://www.hutteman.com/weblog/2003/05/02.html#000079")*,* [*Matt*](http://www.mattberther.com/2003/05/000072.html "http://www.mattberther.com/2003/05/000072.html")*, and* [*I*](http://www.pocketsoap.com/weblog/2003/04/1239.html "http://www.pocketsoap.com/weblog/2003/04/1239.html")*. The revised assembly is available on the* [*IBlogExtension*](http://www.pocketsoap.com/weblog/stories/2003/04/0023.html "http://www.pocketsoap.com/weblog/stories/2003/04/0023.html") *page. I'll be posting versions of Synderilla and Relaxer that support this soon.*
