Individual posts are now [pingback](http://www.hixie.ch/specs/pingback/pingback)-enabled. If you're using a pingback-enabled client, can you try a pingback for this entry? I've tested it with the [test blog](http://www.dummy-blog.org/) and all seems to be well, but there's no substitute for real clients.  
  
I borrowed [Sam Ruby's](http://www.intertwingly.net/blog) idea and included the pingbacks with the comments. Thanks Sam!
