Sam Ruby's [wiki](http://www.intertwingly.net/wiki/pie/RoadMap)Â is documenting an effort to define a syndication format, designed to overcome some of the existing issues with RSS (which I won't get into here!). If a new syndication format grows out of this, [NewsGator](http://www.newsgator.com) will support it (in addition to RSS) in the first release thereafter.
