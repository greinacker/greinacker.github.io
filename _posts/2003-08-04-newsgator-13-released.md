At long last, NewsGator 1.3 is [now available](http://www.newsgator.com/downloads.aspx)!

![](/gregr/weblog/assets/postImages/ng13pic3.gif)

A few highlights:

- NNTP newsgroup support:

![](/gregr/weblog/assets/postImages/ng13pic2.gif)

- Full posts optionally shown on NewsPage:

![](/gregr/weblog/assets/postImages/ng13pic1.gif)

- Feed wizard, with search and directory support
- Tighter folder integration, supporting renaming and deleting folders
- Offline and draft posting mode
- Multiple profile support
- Plain-text publishing
- Initial Atom support
- Relative link resolution
- Fixed some major stability problems
- Lots of other changes!

The complete change list is on the [download page](http://www.newsgator.com/downloads.aspx).
