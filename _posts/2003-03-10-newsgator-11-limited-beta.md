If you're interested in testing a beta release of NewsGator 1.1, [send me a note](http://www.rassoc.com/gregr/weblog/contact.aspx) to that effect. In your note, include the following: OS version and service pack, and Outlook version number (from Help/About). If you are an existing NewsGator customer, please indicate that fact.

I'm not going to publish a feature list yet, but if you've been needing support for authentication, new message notification, or per-feed update intervals, or been having problems with duplicate posts or your proxy server, you might want to sign up. There are numerous other changes and bug fixes as well.
